online learning just got an upgrade

bovine is aimed at making online learning simple, not scary

blazing fast

bovine has one thing that makes it unique from the others: speed. Unsurpassed speed is achieved by eleminating the need for a file-based webserver and instead using a server-side app built specifically for bovine.

bleeding-edge technology

bovine is built on top of AngularJS, node.js and mongoDB, with Jade implemented to allow simple, flexible templating and theming support
